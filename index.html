We are a group of researchers working with of bioinformatics, AI, and cheminformatics.

We are based in the Applied Mathematics and Statistics division in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Chalmers University of Technology. Led by Professor Erik Kristiansson, our group uses methods from bioinfmatics, machine learning and artificial intelligence to understand antibiotic resistence and chemical hazards.
