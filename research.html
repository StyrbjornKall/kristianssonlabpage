Research

We work with bioinformatics, chemoinformatics, and AI to understand and interpret complex molecular data.

The group specializes in using computational methods to address global challenges, including antimicrobial resistance, environmental chemical toxicity, and biodiversity. We are highly interdisciplinary, with expertise in data science, statistics, machine learning, microbiology, and molecular biology. The group is based at the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Chalmers University of Technology and is led by Professor Erik Kristiansson.
